Checklist for eZ article
-----------------------

admin:

- Create a new article, add images upload files, do not publish it.
  Check that the article comes up in the unpublished list
- Publish the article 
  Check that the article, images and files are accessable on the user site.
- Edit the article check upload new files and new images.
  Check that all the data on the user side is updated and that the correct 
  images and files are shown.
- Create a new article category
  Check that all user pages are updated and cache is cleared.
- Edit that article category.
  Check that all user pages are updated and cache is cleared.
- Search after the article in the admin.
  Check that you find it with keywords in name and body.
